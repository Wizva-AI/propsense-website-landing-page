About Prestige Glenbrook

Prestige Glenbrook is a new residential enclave of towers that are picturesquely set amidst beautifully landscaped gardens, rising tall and proud in the heart of Whitefield. Offering a choice of two, three, and four-bed homes, it is a haven of peace and comfort that is secluded from the bustle of the city, but at the same time, conveniently accessible from the IT hubs of Whitefield and Marathahalli-Sarjapur Outer Ring Road.

Connectivity: Close to major IT parks like Prestige Tech Park and Cessna Business Park, as well as malls like Phoenix Marketcity and Brookefield Mall.
