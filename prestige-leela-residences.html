About Prestige Leela Residences

✨ INSPIRED BY MYSORE ROYAL PALACE

Prestige Leela Residences is a fully air-conditioned ultra-luxury complex set amidst lush landscaped gardens, in a style of architecture reminiscent of the Mysore Royal Palace. The 88-residence development is centrally located on Old Airport Road, Bangalore, and has been tastefully designed to echo luxury blended with regal ambience.

These 88 units include 4 palatial duplex penthouses that boast intricate detailing and ornate features such as domes, arches, and decorated ceilings. The ten-storeyed building is truly a one-of-its-kind lifestyle experience, emulating luxuriant exoticism through awe-inspiring features such as overhanging eaves, vaulted roofs, and domed chhatris.

Connectivity: Prime location with proximity to landmarks like KGA (Karnataka Golf Association) and Manipal Hospital.
